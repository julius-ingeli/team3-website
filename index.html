Rules and Regulations

To ensure a harmonious and enjoyable experience for all attendees, please familiarize yourself with the following rules and regulations:

1. Respect for Others

• Treat fellow festival-goers, staff, and performers with kindness and consideration.

• Avoid disruptive behavior that may interfere with others' enjoyment of the music.

2. Festival Grounds

• Please keep the festival grounds clean and dispose of waste in designated bins.

• Follow designated pathways and respect restricted areas for the safety of all.

3. Alcohol and Substance Use

• Alcohol is allowed only in designated areas and must be consumed responsibly.

• The use of illegal substances is strictly prohibited.

4. Photography and Recording

• Personal photography and recording for non-commercial use are generally permitted.

• Professional equipment and commercial photography require prior authorization from festival organizers.

5. Seating Areas

• Respect designated seating areas and be considerate of other attendees' space.

• Do not reserve seats for extended periods, especially in high-demand areas.

6. Safety Measures

• Follow all posted safety guidelines and emergency procedures.

• In case of emergency, follow instructions from festival staff and security personnel.

7. Pets

• For the safety and comfort of all attendees, pets are not allowed on the festival grounds (except for service animals).

8. Ticketing and Wristbands

• Festival entry requires a valid ticket or wristband. Ensure yours is visible at all times.

• Tickets are non-transferable. Any attempt to transfer, sell, or alter tickets may result in removal from the festival.

9. Weather Preparedness

• Be prepared for varying weather conditions. Bring appropriate clothing and accessories.

• Follow announcements for any weather-related updates or changes to the schedule.

10. Compliance with Laws

• Attendees must comply with all local laws and regulations.

• Any behavior that violates the law may result in removal from the festival grounds and possible legal action.

11. Festival Etiquette

• Please refrain from talking loudly during performances.

• Turn off or silence electronic devices to minimize disruptions.

By attending Crescent City Jazz Jam, you agree to abide by these rules and regulations. Festival organizers reserve the right to remove any individual who violates these guidelines without refund. Thank you for your cooperation, and enjoy the jazz festivities!

About Us

Organizers


Sarah Davis - Festival Director

A seasoned event planner with a deep appreciation for jazz, Sarah Davis brings her wealth of experience to the helm of Crescent City Jazz Jam. Her vision is to create an inclusive space where the beauty and diversity of jazz can be celebrated, fostering a sense of community among music enthusiasts.

Rodriguez - Artistic Curator

As an accomplished jazz musician himself, Michael Rodriguez lends his artistic sensibilities to curate a lineup that showcases the rich tapestry of jazz. His commitment to promoting both established legends and emerging talents ensures that every edition of the festival is a journey through the evolution of jazz.

Q: Why Crescent City Jazz Jam?

A:

Our journey began with a simple yet profound belief – that jazz is not just a genre of music; it's a cultural phenomenon that deserves to be celebrated. The founders of Crescent City Jazz Jam recognized the need for a platform where jazz could be experienced in all its forms, from the traditional to the avant-garde, creating a space for artists and audiences alike to connect through the universal language of music.
